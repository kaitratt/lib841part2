Class Highlights

  • At our school, we get to chose a mascot for our class. This year we were the Ratterman's Rattlesnakes! Here is a picture of us together at our first field day! We won tug o' war!
  • Lastly, the picture in the middle was from the last day of school. I have a tradition of buying my students sunglasses because their future is sooo bright all they need is shades!!
